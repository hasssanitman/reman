Case Study: Ojrat

Ojrat is a comprehensive payroll management system.

Companies and Organizations

  • Industry: All Industries
Ojrat is a payroll management system that receives raw data of employees' performance and attendance in the form of Excel files, and based on the formulas defined by administrators, it generates employees' payslips for online access.

Project Requirements

  • Data input through Excel file uploads
  • Calculation and generation of payslips
  • Multiple formula definitions for payslip generation with formula selection during processing
  • Employee registration and online payslip access
  • Employment orders and salary deduction letters for bank facilities
