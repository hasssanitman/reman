Case Study: Carbon

Carbon is a store builder system for setting up online and offline businesses.

iBrand Agency

  • Industry: E-commerce
  • Size: 30+
  • Website: carbonfy.ir
iBrand is a digital marketing agency that advises online businesses in the fields of fashion and clothing, and online stores on social media and web presence.

Project Requirements

  • Simplicity and high speed
  • Tracking and instant issuance of postal tracking codes
  • Abandoned cart tracking
  • Shopping cart reservation for a certain period
  • Connecting to accounting and warehousing software
  • Cross-sell functionality
  • Wallet and cashback features

Project Overview

Carbon is a dedicated API-based store builder created in 2021 in response to the needs of our customers and market - growing businesses looking for an effective web presence. As this need grew, we decided to use our marketing and technical experience to produce a comprehensive product to meet this need.

The Challenge & The Approach

Most of iBrand's customers are active in the fields of fashion and clothing, and existing store builders were not able to meet their needs. Due to the many years of experience of the iBrand consulting team and complete understanding of the specific needs of these businesses, we decided to create a dedicated Carbon store builder system with the aim of covering all the needs of an online clothing store or any type of online business.

Carbon is SEO-friendly. One of the challenges of Carbon as an API project was SEO. We used SSR (Server-Side Rendering) technology, schema markup, sitemaps, and developed specialized tools for webmasters. In addition, when businesses migrate from WordPress to Carbon, their SEO is preserved and links remain unbroken.

In the clothing business, a product with different features is sold at different prices. In Carbon, it is possible to create any type of product with unlimited different features and separate warehouse counts. Discount coupons can be applied to all variations or only specific ones with adjustable conditions.

Using the Carbon warehouse management system, you can manage the store's warehouse simultaneously for offline and online sales.

Carbon can be connected to conventional accounting systems.

With cooperation from the post office, it is possible to issue instant tracking codes in Carbon.

Many customers want to purchase a product and wait until another product is available, then complete their purchase and have the package sent to them. We have developed the possibility of reserving shopping carts in Carbon, and the store manager can determine the duration of the reservation and activate this feature.

Cross-selling is very important for marketing, and this feature is also available in Carbon.

Cashback and electronic wallet are other features offered by Carbon.
