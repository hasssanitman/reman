Case Study: Carbon

Carbon is a store builder system for setting up online and offline businesses.

iBrand Agency

  • Industry: E-commerce
  • Size: 30+
  • Website: carbonfy.ir
iBrand is a digital marketing agency that advises online businesses in the field of fashion and clothing and online stores on social media and the web.

Project Requirements

  • Simplicity and high speed
  • Tracking and instant issuance of postal tracking code
  • Abandoned cart tracking
  • Shopping cart reservation for a certain period
  • Connecting to accounting and warehousing software
  • Cross sell
  • Wallet and Cash back

Project Overview

Carbon is a dedicated API base store builder created in 2021 in response to the needs of our customers and market, growing businesses looking for an effective web presence. As this need grew, we decided to use our marketing and technical experience to produce a product to comprehensively answer this need.

The Challenge & The Approach

Most of iBrand's customers are active in the field of fashion and clothing, and the existing store builders were not able to meet their needs. Due to the many years of experience of the iBrand consulting team and the complete understanding of the specific needs of these businesses, we decided to create a dedicated carbon store builder system with the aim of covering all the needs of an online clothing store or any type of online business.

Carbon is SEO friendly. One of the challenges of Carbon as an API project was SEO. We used SSR technology, schema, sitemap and many other things to solve this problem. Also, if they migrate from WordPress to Carbon, their SEO will not be damaged and the links will not be broken.

In the clothing business, a product with different features is sold at different prices. In Carbon, it is possible to create any type of product with infinite different features and separate warehouse counts. which can be applied to all of them or only one of them with the adjustable conditions of the discount coupon.

Using the carbon warehouse management system, you can manage the store's warehouse simultaneously for offline and online sales.

Carbon can be connected to conventional accounting systems.

With the cooperation of the post office, it is possible to issue an instant tracking code in carbon.

Many customers want to buy a product and wait until another product is available and then complete their purchase and the package is sent to them. We have developed the possibility of reserving the shopping cart in Carbon, and the store manager can determine the duration of the reservation and activate this possibility.

Cross-selling is very important for marketing, and this feature is also available in Carbon.

Cashback and electronic wallet are other things offered by Carbon.
