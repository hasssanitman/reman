PROJECTS
Yargar

Yargar is a system for managing and monitoring the distribution of agricultural inputs. With Yargar, inputs can be tracked from the time they are allocated to a province until they reach the consumer. The most important goal of Yargar is to prevent the smuggling of inputs and create a secondary market.

Yargar is used in Golestan province by more than 210,000 farmers and ranchers and the annual distribution volume is more than 500,000 tons.
